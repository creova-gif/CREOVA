## SQUARESPACE IMPLEMENTATION TIPS

1. **Use Header Tags Properly:**
   - H1 for main page title (one per page)
   - H2 for major sections
   - H3 for subsections

2. **Add Meta Descriptions:**
   - Go to Pages → Click gear icon → SEO tab
   - Add custom meta title and description for EVERY page

3. **Create a Blog:**
   - Publish 1-2 posts per month with your target keywords
   - Link internally to service pages

4. **Enable SSL & Mobile Optimization:**
   - Already standard on Squarespace, but confirm it's active

5. **Submit Sitemap to Google:**
   - Your sitemap is at: yourdomain.com/sitemap.xml
   - Submit via Google Search Console

6. **Use Squarespace's Built-in SEO Tools:**
   - Enable SEO-friendly URLs
   - Add alt text to all images
   - Connect Google Analytics

7. **Local SEO (Google Business Profile):**
   - Create a Google Business Profile
   - Use consistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone)
   - Encourage client reviews
